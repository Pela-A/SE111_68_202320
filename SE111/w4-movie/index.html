In a hypothetical world where all people are cars, Lightning McQueen is a rookie racer trying to win his first piston cup. While Mack, the truck responsible for bringing Lightning to his races is driving in the night he doozes off due to some hooligans leaving McQueen stranded in a small unknown town called Radiator Springs. It is up to McQueen to make it out of Radiator Springs and evolve as a racer in order to win the Piston Cup.
